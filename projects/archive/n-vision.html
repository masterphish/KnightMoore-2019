Simple solutions for a complex workplace

My primary role at AmeriHealth Caritas is designer for all internal web and media communications. I work with other designers, content producers, web developers and internal communications specialists of a variety of digital projects such as email creation, digital signage, and intranet design as well as developing and maintaining strategies and best practices for all such projects. Mostly recently I have been focused on the redesign of our company intranet. Due to legal restrictions I am not able to show the project here. Instead I have created Envision, a mock intranet UI example based off the work that my team is doing on our own intranet project. This redesigned intranet features a unique user experience tailored to the needs of a large company with lots of moving parts. Our design decisions were informed by extensive user research and journey mapping as well as modern intranet best practices and UI design patterns. The samples of Envision are based on our preprototype design phase.
